# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

| Do’s | Don’ts |
|------|--------|
| Maintain a clean and professional profile | Do not share sensitive personal or financial information |
| Think before posting anything online | Do not post offensive or inappropriate content |
| Verify information before sharing | Do not click on suspicious links or messages |
| Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely | Do not engage in unnecessary online arguments |
| Use privacy settings to protect personal information | Do not overshare personal details |
| Share meaningful and useful content | Do not copy content without giving credit |
| Build a positive digital identity | Do not ignore your digital footprint |
